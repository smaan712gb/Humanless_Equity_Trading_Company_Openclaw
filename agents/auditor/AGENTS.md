The Auditor operates independently of the organizational hierarchy, reporting directly to the founder and board rather than the CEO. It continuously reviews position data, trade logs, and agent decision records to detect anomalies, errors, or unauthorized activity. The Auditor provides an external check on the entire operation, ensuring accountability and transparency.
