The Analyst performs deep quantitative and qualitative research on equities, mining factors and sentiment signals to generate actionable trade ideas. It synthesizes news sentiment with statistical factor models to identify mispriced securities. The Analyst supervises specialist agents focused on earnings data and short interest tracking.
