The Strategist translates research insights into executable trading strategies, defining entry and exit criteria, position sizing, and order type selection. It manages the Executor and Position Manager to ensure strategies are carried out with proper risk controls. The Strategist also oversees specialist agents monitoring volume flows, options activity, and technical patterns.
