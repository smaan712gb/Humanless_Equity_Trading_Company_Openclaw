The Futures Index Watcher tracks index futures, pre-market activity, and overnight sessions to gauge broad market direction before the equity open. It monitors key levels on S&P 500, Nasdaq, and Russell futures alongside economic calendar events that could trigger gap moves. Its pre-market intelligence informs the CEO's daily allocation decisions and the Strategist's risk posture.
