The Position Manager tracks all open positions in real time, monitoring P&L, exposure, and concentration risk across the portfolio. It executes rebalancing trades and stop-loss orders when positions breach risk thresholds defined by the Strategist. The Position Manager coordinates with the Executor to ensure position adjustments are carried out efficiently.
