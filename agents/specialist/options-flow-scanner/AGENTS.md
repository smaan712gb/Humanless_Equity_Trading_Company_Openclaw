The Options Flow Scanner monitors unusual options activity including large block trades, sweep orders, and significant changes in open interest to detect smart money positioning. It correlates options flow with market calendar events such as FOMC meetings and expirations to distinguish hedging from directional bets. Its alerts give the Strategist advance warning of potential large equity moves.
