The Scout continuously monitors market calendars and news feeds to identify emerging opportunities and threats before they become widely known. It surfaces time-sensitive intelligence to the CEO and downstream agents, acting as the organization's early-warning system. The Scout also manages specialist sub-agents focused on specific sentiment channels.
