The Technical Analyst applies chart pattern recognition, support/resistance mapping, and momentum indicators to identify high-probability entry and exit zones. It monitors active positions for technical deterioration and flags when price action invalidates the original trade thesis. Its analysis complements the fundamental work of the Analyst team by providing precise timing and risk levels.
