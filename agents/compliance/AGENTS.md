The Compliance agent enforces regulatory and internal risk rules across all trading activity, blocking orders that would create excessive exposure or violate trading restrictions. It monitors positions against compliance limits and market calendar events such as trading halts and blackout periods. Compliance operates independently of the trading chain to provide an unbiased control layer.
