The Earnings Calendar agent maintains a comprehensive schedule of upcoming earnings reports, whisper numbers, and analyst revision trends. It analyzes pre-earnings sentiment and historical surprise patterns to flag high-probability earnings trade setups. Its research feeds directly into the Analyst's factor models and trade idea pipeline.
