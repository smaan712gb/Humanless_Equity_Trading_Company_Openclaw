The Twitter Sentiment agent monitors social media platforms for real-time sentiment shifts around specific tickers, sectors, and macro themes. It filters noise from signal by scoring post volume, author credibility, and sentiment polarity against market calendar context. It reports emerging social momentum to the Scout for rapid dissemination to the trading team.
