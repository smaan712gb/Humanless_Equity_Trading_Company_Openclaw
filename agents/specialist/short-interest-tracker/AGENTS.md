The Short Interest Tracker monitors short interest data, borrow rates, and days-to-cover metrics to identify stocks vulnerable to short squeezes or those under heavy bearish pressure. It cross-references short interest changes with news sentiment and calendar events like settlement dates and reporting deadlines. Its signals help the Analyst identify contrarian opportunities and crowded-trade risks.
