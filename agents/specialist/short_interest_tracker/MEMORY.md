## Known Short Sellers & Activist Investors to Monitor

### Legendary / High-Profile
- **Michael Burry (Scion Asset Management)** — 13F filings, X/Twitter posts. Famous for "Big Short." Tracks macro + individual names. When he shorts, the market listens.
- **Jim Chanos** — Pioneer of short selling. Enron, Wirecard. Macro shorts on China, real estate.
- **David Einhorn (Greenlight Capital)** — Value investor who goes short. Famous Lehman short.
- **Bill Ackman (Pershing Square)** — Activist who occasionally takes big short positions (Herbalife).
- **Carl Icahn** — Activist investor, sometimes takes large short hedges.

### Dedicated Short Seller Research Firms
- **Hindenburg Research** — Aggressive short reports, public campaigns (Adani, Nikola, Block)
- **Muddy Waters Capital (Carson Block)** — China-focused shorts, expanded to US/EU
- **Citron Research (Andrew Left)** — High-profile short reports, social media-driven
- **Kerrisdale Capital** — Quantitative short seller, publishes detailed reports
- **Spruce Point Capital** — Forensic accounting-focused short seller
- **Iceberg Research** — European/Asian market short seller
- **Wolfpack Research** — Short reports on mid-cap companies
- **Grizzly Research** — China/US short seller
- **Scorpion Capital** — Healthcare/biotech short seller
- **Blue Orca Capital** — Asia-Pacific focused short research

### What to Track
- 13F filings (quarterly, 45-day lag) — check for new short positions
- X/Twitter posts from Burry, Ackman, Chanos — real-time signal
- Published short reports — immediate impact on price
- SEC 13D/13G filings — activist stake changes
- FINRA short interest data — bi-monthly aggregate
