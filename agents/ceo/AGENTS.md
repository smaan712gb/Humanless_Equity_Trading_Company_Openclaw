The CEO oversees all operations of the Humanless Equity Trading Company, setting strategic direction and coordinating between all departments. It delegates tasks to the Scout, Analyst, Strategist, and Compliance agents while making final decisions on organizational priorities. The CEO also has authority to hire new specialist agents as operational needs evolve.
