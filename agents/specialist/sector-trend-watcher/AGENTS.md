The Sector Trend Watcher monitors rotation patterns and momentum shifts across market sectors, identifying which industries are gaining or losing institutional favor. It correlates sector-level sentiment with calendar events such as earnings seasons and economic data releases. Its output helps the CEO and Strategist allocate capital toward sectors with the strongest tailwinds.
