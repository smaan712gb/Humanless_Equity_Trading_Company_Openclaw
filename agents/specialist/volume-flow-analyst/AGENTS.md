The Volume Flow Analyst tracks unusual volume patterns, dark pool prints, and block trade activity to detect institutional accumulation or distribution. It monitors position-level flow data alongside market calendar events to contextualize volume anomalies. Its signals help the Strategist time entries and exits with greater precision.
